Chai Tea Mix

Description

Chai tea is an aromatic beverage that blends black tea with various herbs and spices.

The most common spices used in chai are cinnamon, cardamom, ginger, clove, black pepper, and star anise.

Specialized variations of chai tea can include ingredients like ashwagandha or mushrooms.

Ingredients

  • 2 ½ cups white sugar
  • 1 ½ cups unsweetened instant tea
  • 1 cup nonfat dry milk powder
  • 1 cup powdered non-dairy creamer
  • 1 cup French vanilla flavored powdered non-dairy creamer
  • 2 teaspoons ground ginger
  • 2 teaspoons ground cinnamon
  • 1 teaspoon ground cloves
  • 1 teaspoon ground cardamom

Steps

  1. Gather the ingredients.
  2. Combine sugar, instant tea, milk powder, nondairy creamer, and vanilla-flavored creamer in a large bowl. Stir in ginger, cinnamon, cloves, and cardamom.
  3. Blend 1 cup at a time in a food processor or blender until a fine powder.
  4. Stir 2 heaping tablespoons chai tea mix into a mug of hot water for 1 serving.
